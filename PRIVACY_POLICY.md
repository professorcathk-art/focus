# Privacy Policy for Focus

**Last Updated**: December 13, 2024

## Introduction

Focus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- User ID (automatically generated)

### Content You Create
- Ideas and notes that you record or type
- Audio recordings (processed for transcription and immediately deleted)
- Categories and tags you assign to your ideas
- To-do list items

### Usage Information
- App usage patterns (to improve our service)
- Error logs (to fix bugs)

## How We Use Your Information

- **To provide our service**: Store and organize your ideas
- **To improve the app**: Analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
- **To authenticate**: Verify your identity and secure your account

## Data Storage and Security

- **Encrypted Storage**: All data is stored securely using Supabase (encrypted database)
- **Audio Processing**: Audio recordings are transcribed using AI and immediately deleted after processing
- **Secure Authentication**: We use Supabase Auth for secure user authentication
- **Data Retention**: Your data is retained until you delete your account

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase**: User authentication and database storage
  - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- **AIMLAPI**: AI-powered categorization and search
  - Privacy Policy: Check AIMLAPI documentation
- **Vercel**: Backend API hosting
  - Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your stored data
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- **Export**: Export your ideas and data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
